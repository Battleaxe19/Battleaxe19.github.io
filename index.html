WHAT WE DO

We invite boss babes of all levels to experiences that accelerate your personal and professional success. Through events, small-group mentoring, personalized introductions and more, we give you what you need most: face-time with the people who will change the game of your career. We handpick pros from all areas of business to support you in three ways:

  1. 1) Events: Sparking connections & building community

  2. 2) Learning: Sharing success stories, actionable tools and tactics

  3. 3) Mentors: Aligning you with badass people for advice and strategic guidance

WHY WE DO IT

Success of female leaders is so important, but existing support structures and resources are simply not moving enough capable and deserving women into leadership. We are reclaiming the feminine and carving new routes to foster a fresh generation of female leaders.

THE INFLUENCER COLLECTIVE

A special peer-to-peer mentoring group for women

We all know that building a killer social network is one of the most critical choices you'll make to get ahead in business, but we also know how much time and effort creating that network on your own can take. So we've done the work for you.

Joining the Influencer Collective gets you unlimited access to all content on our site including full-length videos and articles.

PLUS

  • 4 curated peer-peer mentoring circles of 10-12 influencers per year (dinners, activities & adventures)
  • 2 premier mentoring circles with a figure at the top of her game (think CEOs, founders & trailblazers from all over the US)
  • A professionally crafted spotlight feature in our community media
  • Personalized connections to mentors and other influencers
